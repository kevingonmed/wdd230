Hick's Law

Google

Google Homepage

Google keeps the decisions required to enter a keyword to a minimum by eliminating any additional content that could distract from the act of typing a keyword or require additional decision-making.

Fitt's Law

Friv

Friv.com

On friv when you move the mouse to any game, the game logo gets bigger, making it easier for the user to select and watch the logo of the game.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon

Amazon Homepage

Amazon's homepage employs the rule of thirds, featuring a grid of nine content boxes at the top, a central horizontal box with rotating images, and a repeating pattern of three boxes followed by a horizontal section as you scroll down.
